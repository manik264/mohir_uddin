About Us

Mahir Uddin Shah Hafezia Orphanage Madrasa was established in 1997. It was founded by Mohammad Sheikh Afzal Hossain. He named this institution after his grandfather Mahir Uddin Shah. This institution played an important role in spreading religious education and practicing religion in the area. Currently there is provision of technical training along with education.
Apart from his father, he helped to build this institution and several other institutions like Poschim Goalgihi Center Dangga Govt Primary School,Goaldihi Girl High School, Golam Rahman Shah Fazil Madrasa, Jonab ali Asia charity hospital were established by his father Md. Sheikh Jonab Ali Shah. This family played an important role in the spread of education in the area.

Along with educational programs, there are many recreational programs that keep the students motivated.
